Samsung Galaxy S10

The Samsung Galaxy S10 is a phone by Android manufactured and sold by Samsung Electronics as a part of Samsung's Galaxy "S" line of phones. The Samsung Galaxy S10 is the tenth generation of Samsung's Galaxy "S" line of phones. The Samsung Galaxy S10 was an important milestone because it was the 10th aniversary of Samsung's Galaxy "S" line of phones. The Samsung Galaxy S10 was revealed to be released at the Samsung Galaxy Unpacked in 2019 it was a press event and it was held on the twentyth of Febuary in 2019. Not long after the Samsung Galaxy unpacked 2019 press event the Samsung Galaxy S10 was shipped in certain places such as the United states and Australia on the sixth of March in 2019, two days later the Samsung Galaxy S10 was released worldwide for all to buy and enjoy using. The Samsung Galaxy S10 has the mmany varients more varients than most of the phones in Samsung's Galaxy "S" line of phones. The varients are Samsung Galaxy S10e, Samsung Galaxy S10+, Samsung Galaxy S10 5G, Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite all of these varients costed differant amount with the regular Samsung Galaxy S10 being the cheapest one and the Samsung Galaxy S105G being the most expensive one. On the release of the Samsung Galaxy S10 the cost was about 600-700 dollars while the Samsung Galaxy S10+ costed about 700-800 dollars, the Samsung Galaxy S105G costed about 1100-1300 dollars being the most expensive phone they had made at the time. The biggest commpetitior for the Samsung Galaxy S10 was Apple's Iphone 11.
