Samsung Galaxy S III

The Samsung Galaxy SIII is the third phone in Samsungs "S" line of phones desgnined, deverloped, and sold by Samsung electronics, the Samsung Galaxys SIII was announced in 2012 that it would be released and not long after the announcement Samsung released the Samsung Galaxy SIII, and Samsung has sold over 80 million Samsung Galaxy SIIIs as of the 18th of october 2022 making it the most sold Samsung phone in the "S" phone series. The Samsung Galaxy SIII has better, new, and helpful software features along with expanded hardware and a redesigned look/feel from the previous Samsung Galaxy phone (Samsung Galaxy SII which was released the previous year) The Samsung Galaxy SIII features an intelligent personal assistant or S Voice, the ability to track peoples eyes via eye tracker, and way more storage the the prevoius Samsung S series phones. It was announced that the Samsung Galaxy SIII would have a wireless charging option but when the Samsung Galaxy SIII came out it did not have that feature. The Samsung Galaxy SIII is 4.9 inchs (120 milimeter) and the Samsung Galaxy SIII comes with different processors and Ram capacity, and it also comes with 4G LTE support. The Samsung Galaxy SIII was launched using Android 4.0.4 nicknamed "Ice Cream Sandwich", then was updated to Android 4.3 nicknamed "Jelly Bean", and offer the capibility to update to 4.4.2 but only on phones that have at least 2GB of RAM and it has the nickname "KitKat". The Samsung Galaxy SIII was released in 28 of the Middle Eastern and European countries on may twenty ninth of May in 2012.
